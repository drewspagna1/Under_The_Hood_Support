Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Under The Hood does not collect your name, email address, or any information that identifies you personally. The app uses anonymous sessions only.

Who we are

Under The Hood is an iOS app developed by Under The Hood. Questions about this policy can be directed to privacy@underthehoodapp.com.

What we collect

When you use Under The Hood, we collect the following data anonymously:

  • Anonymous session ID — a random identifier generated on first launch, not linked to your identity or Apple ID.
  • Quote data — repair types, quoted prices, and ZIP code (if provided) submitted during a scan. This is stored without any personal identifiers.
  • Usage data — basic app usage events (e.g. number of scans) to help us understand how the app is used. No personally identifying information is attached.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, Apple ID, location beyond ZIP code, photos, or any other personal information.

How we use this data

  • To provide and improve the core quote analysis feature
  • To build a proprietary pricing database that improves fair-price estimates over time
  • To understand aggregate usage patterns and improve the app

We do not use your data to serve advertisements and we do not sell your data to third parties.

Third-party services

Under The Hood uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy practices:

  • Google Gemini API — repair quote images and text are sent to Google's Gemini API for analysis. Google's Privacy Policy applies to data processed by this service.
  • Supabase — anonymous quote data is stored in Supabase. Supabase's Privacy Policy applies.
  • RevenueCat — in-app purchase management. RevenueCat's Privacy Policy applies. RevenueCat may assign its own anonymous identifier to your device for purchase tracking.

Data retention

Anonymous quote data is retained indefinitely to improve pricing accuracy. Because data is not linked to any personal identifier, we have no way to locate or delete a specific user's data upon request. If you would like to discuss data retention, contact us at privacy@underthehoodapp.com.

Children

Under The Hood is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects when the most recent changes were made. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions or concerns about this policy? Email us at privacy@underthehoodapp.com.
