Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Under The Hood does not collect your name, email address, device ID, or any information that identifies you. No account is required. The only data we process is content you choose to submit.

Who we are

Under The Hood is an iOS app developed by Under The Hood. Questions about this policy can be directed to privacy@underthehoodapp.com.

What we collect

The only data Under The Hood processes is content you voluntarily submit — specifically:

  • Repair quote photos or text — the image or typed line items you submit for analysis.
  • OBD-II codes or symptom descriptions — if you use the diagnosis feature.
  • Vehicle info and ZIP code — optional fields you can fill in to improve pricing accuracy.

In Apple's App Store privacy categories this falls under Photos and Videos (quote images sent for analysis) and Other Usage Data (anonymous quote and pricing data). Neither is linked to your identity or used for tracking. We do not collect your name, email, phone number, Apple ID, device identifiers, location, or browsing history.

How we use this data

Submitted quote data is stored anonymously to provide the analysis result and, in aggregate, to improve fair-price estimates over time. We do not use your data for advertising and we do not sell it to third parties.

Third-party services

Under The Hood uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy practices:

  • Google Gemini API — quote photos and text are sent to Google's Gemini API for analysis. Google's Privacy Policy applies.
  • Supabase — anonymous quote data is stored in Supabase. Supabase's Privacy Policy applies.
  • RevenueCat — in-app purchase management. RevenueCat's Privacy Policy applies.

Data retention

Submitted quote data is retained anonymously to improve pricing accuracy over time. Because no personal identifiers are stored, we have no way to locate or delete a specific user's submissions. Contact us at privacy@underthehoodapp.com with any questions.

Children

Under The Hood is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects when the most recent changes were made. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions or concerns about this policy? Email us at privacy@underthehoodapp.com.
